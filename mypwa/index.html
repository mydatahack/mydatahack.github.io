Cross Platform

Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) is a technology to create native-like mobile app experience with a webstite. As the app is just a website, it works on any platform.

Simple Installation

With PWAs, we do not need an app store to deliver apps to mobile phones. When you visit the site that supports PWA with your mobile phone, it will prompt you to add home, which installs the app.

Better Developer Experience

Don't waste time & energy on packaging your app. The major app stores are too slow for PWA development. Our registration process is just to enter your app's URL, which makes more sense for PWAs. Create your app and register it. Your app can be discovered by millions of users immediately.
